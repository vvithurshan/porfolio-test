Molecular Insights into PHC Phase Separation

Aug 2024 – May 2026
  • Investigated LLPS and SAM domain dimerization in Polyhomeotic (PRC1) proteins using atomistic and coarse-grained MD simulations.

Jan 2024 – Nov 2024

Sep 2022 – Dec 2023
  • Developed a hybrid GNN + protein language model using structural and evolutionary features.
